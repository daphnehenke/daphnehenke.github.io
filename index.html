About Me

I began my studies at the University of Nebraska-Omaha in the fall of 2023 and I am projected to graduate in May 2027. Art has always been a passion of mine, as well as teaching. I have thrived through UNO's Teacher ED courses, but UNO's studio art classes have especially stengthened both my skill and my ambition as an aspiring art educator. Since my first foundations studio art class in the spring of 2024, I have had the opportunity to challenge myself and grow as an artist and I will forever be thankful to my professors and instructors in the art department at UNO. These amazing men and women have helped me to become more confident in my artwork but also have challenged my abilities as an arist which has, in turn , helped me grow as a student, classmate, and aspiring artist and teacher. Thanks for stopping by!
